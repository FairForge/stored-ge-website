About stored.ge

Our Mission

Stored, a FairForge company, seeks to democratize enterprise-grade cloud storage by eliminating the complexity and excessive costs that plague traditional providers. We believe that powerful storage infrastructure should be accessible to everyone, from individual developers to large enterprises, without requiring deep pockets or specialized expertise. By combining intelligent storage orchestration with transparent pricing, we're building the platform that makes enterprise storage as simple as consumer services. Our vision is to become the default storage layer for the next generation of applications, enabling builders to focus on innovation rather than infrastructure management.
